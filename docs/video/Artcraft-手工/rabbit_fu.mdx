# Rabbit Fu

## Children's English Craft: Chinese New Year Zodiac - Rabbit Fu

In this video, with the theme of Chinese New Year, we will lead children in making a Chinese New Year craft: Rabbit Fu. This fun craft, combined with an all-English context, helps cultivate children's English speaking and listening skills while significantly increasing their interest in learning English.

Through this video, children can not only develop their English language abilities but also exercise their fine motor skills. It is suitable for preschoolers and elementary school children to watch.
