# From Head to Toe

## Children's English Picture Book Teaching: From Head to Toe | Read Aloud | Classic Picture Book Reading | Grandpa Karl

In this video, we bring you Grandpa Karl's classic English picture book "From Head to Toe," translated as《从头到脚》in Chinese.
This is an interactive picture book that gets children moving. The illustrations are vibrant and engaging! The simple and repetitive sentences are also beneficial for children to practice reading along.
Teacher Katherine is very approachable, with authentic pronunciation and expressive body language. Let's follow her to read the picture book and learn body parts and animal vocabulary together!
