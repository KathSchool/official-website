# Walking In The Forest

## Singing a Nursery Rhyme While Walking In The Forest

Hi~ In this video, we have the second part of the "Walking" nursery rhyme series: "Walking In The Forest."
Key words: forest, deer, owl, rabbit, woodpecker, skunk, walk, stomp, jump, skip.
For those who have watched the first part "Walking In The Jungle," you can continue learning new conversational expressions in this familiar melody!
