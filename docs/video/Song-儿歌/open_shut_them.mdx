# Open Shut Them

## [English Nursery Rhyme Teaching] Open Shut Them | Super Simple Song Classic Nursery Rhyme | Teaching Antonyms

In this video, we bring children the classic English nursery rhyme "Open Shut Them" from SSS. We hope that children can sing along and learn and review expressions of English antonyms.
Katherine, the teacher, has also recorded a picture book teaching video on the theme of Opposites, and the link is provided below:
[Opposites Picture Book Teaching](https://www.bilibili.com/video/BV1fY4y1S7RY/?spm_id_from=333.999.0.0&vd_source=078ea57a25ec7de093a9df58a55bc784)
