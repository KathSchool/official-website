## Introduction

In this video, we bring you the classic nursery rhyme "One Little Finger" by SSS.
It's a song to learn the English expressions for body parts, with catchy lyrics and melody, perfect for little ones starting their English learning journey!
Key vocabulary: finger, head, nose, chin, arm, leg, foot.

Follow [Kath School](https://space.bilibili.com/20797715) for a joyful English enlightenment journey with your children!
