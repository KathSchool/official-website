## Introduction

As requested by dear parents, this video brings ocean animals-themed nursery rhymes for children! The song is "Down In The Deep Blue Sea" from Super Simple Song.
The latter part of the video is a vocabulary learning and repeating section. It's recommended for kids to watch and follow along.
After repeating, you can sing the song a few more times to reinforce the learning.
Follow [Kath School](https://space.bilibili.com/20797715) for a joyful English enlightenment journey with your children!
